About Me

Hello, I'm Mahi Abdulhakim Mohammed, hailing from the historical city of Harar, Ethiopia. As a web developer, I find joy in designing and crafting digital solutions. Each line of code I write is an expression of my creativity and a step towards making the internet a more intuitive space. Outside of my professional life, I'm a passionate photographer, capturing life's fleeting moments and transforming them into lasting memories. Soccer is my go-to sport, providing me with both excitement and camaraderie. My love for travel takes me to far-off places, allowing me to experience diverse cultures and perspectives. Each journey enriches me, instilling a deeper appreciation for the world's beauty and its myriad of peoples. Rooted in the rich history of Harar and inspired by modern technology, I am on a continuous path of personal and professional growth, always eager to learn and create.
